ABOUT

Mário D. Sequeira is a junior researcher in the Centre for Social Studies of the University of Coimbra, integrated in the Risk AquaSoil (EAPA_272/2016), a European project financed by INTERREG.Completed his master’s degree in Geosciences in 2013, and currently is a Geology PhD student in the University of Coimbra. His main research fields have been Environmental Geology and Geochemistry, with particular focus on environmental impacts associated with land-use and occupation changes.
